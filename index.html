About us

We are the producers of palm and soy wax, scented and odorless candles, home scents and car scents. We pour every candle with our own hands with a lofty mood and great love for the products we create. We select scents and production materials slowly and thoroughly, and our collections are created with the help of professional product designers. We are here to create cosiness in our customer home, at the same time basing our activity on a slogan WE LIVE COZY !!


Responsiblity

In pursuing our activities, we try to create a sustainable and socially responsible business, we encourage not to waste natural resources, also we participate and support various social actions and projects. We do not produce industrial waste while producing our products, and poor quality production is recycled. In production we aim to use as many natural materials as possible from renewable sources, also we use energy efficiently for production.
